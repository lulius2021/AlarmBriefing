FAQ

What is an API key and why do I need one?

The API key connects your Clawdbot to the AlarmBriefing app. The bot can then create alarms and generate briefings. Generate the key in the app under Settings ‚Üí Bot Connection.

How do I connect my bot?

1. Open the app and go to Settings

2. Click "Generate New Key"

3. Copy the key

4. Set it as ALARMBRIEFING_API_KEY in your Clawdbot

Can I use the app without a bot?

Yes! You can create alarms manually. For audio briefings, you need the bot ‚Äî it generates the content (weather, news, calendar).

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Delete account. All data is immediately and permanently deleted. Alternatively, email julius.deusch@trainq.app.

My data ‚Äî what is stored?

Only what's necessary: email, name, alarm settings. No tracking, no ads. Details in our Privacy Policy.
